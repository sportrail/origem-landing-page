Questions

Everything You Need to Know.
Ask Anyway.

What level of player is ORIGEM designed for? +
ORIGEM accepts players at high school varsity level or above. All applicants go through a short assessment — a video review and a call with our coaching staff. We maintain cohort quality deliberately so the training environment reflects the standard required to genuinely develop your game.
What does "all-inclusive" actually mean? +
Accommodation, all meals, training kit, visa processing support, health insurance, sports nutrition programme, sports psychology sessions, Portuguese language course, gym access, and airport arrival transfers. The only exclusions are flights and personal spending money. ORIGEM Elite additionally includes the masterclass series, college recruiting package, and personal development portfolio. Optional Sportrail Football Education courses are available as an add-on at separate cost.
How does the Sports Nutrition Programme work? +
On arrival, you undergo a full nutritional baseline assessment with a qualified sports nutritionist. A personalised meal plan is developed around your position, training load, and body composition goals. Weekly check-ins track progress and adjust the plan through the season. All meals provided at the academy reflect your plan. Recovery nutrition protocols are built into your match and training day schedules.
How does Sports Psychology work within ORIGEM? +
You receive regular one-to-one sessions with a qualified mental performance coach throughout the year. Sessions cover focus, resilience under pressure, confidence, identity, and the psychological demands of competitive football. Group sessions on team dynamics and performance mindset are also woven into the programme calendar — so the mental game is trained as seriously as the technical game.
What are the Performance Analysis and 1v1 Add-Ons? +
Performance Analysis gives you individual video review sessions with a dedicated analyst — match footage, metrics, heatmaps, and position-specific breakdowns delivered weekly. The 1v1 Technical Training Add-On provides private sessions with a position specialist coach to address your most specific technical needs. Both are booked at the point of enrolment and integrated into your weekly schedule from the start of the programme.
Will players get to play matches during the programme? +
Yes — through friendly matches against visiting academies, touring teams, and other international programmes throughout the season. This is where your match footage is built and your development is tested under real game conditions. Players are not registered with Portuguese clubs on arrival. However, the very best-performing players within the cohort may earn the opportunity to train with our partner club outside programme hours — a chance that has to be earned through the quality of your work on the pitch.
How does the Sportrail Football Education option work? +
Sportrail Football Education courses are entirely optional — available to any ORIGEM player who wants to complement their playing year with deeper football knowledge. Courses cover topics including coaching methodology, performance analysis, sports science, and football management. They're delivered online and fully flexible, so they fit around your training and match schedule. You can take as little or as much as you want. There's no obligation — but for players who want to understand the game at a deeper level, it's an opportunity that simply doesn't exist on any other gap year programme.
Does the gap year affect my NCAA college eligibility? +
No — a gap year does not affect your NCAA eligibility provided you follow standard eligibility guidelines around amateur status. Our team has guided dozens of players through this process successfully. We advise on your specific situation during the application call and can connect you with our network of college recruiting advisors who specialise in European pathway players.
